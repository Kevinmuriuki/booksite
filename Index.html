This is a book written by one of the world's most highly respected leadership expert, Robin Sharma.

In this book he shows you how to claim that power of leading without a title and transform your life - and the world around you - in the process.

Brake Davis, is the character in the book that shares with us his life transforming story all through the book.
He shares his background life in the first chapter with us. He had a nice childhood life which transformed after his parents were killed at his twenties. He struggled with the troumer and eventually joined the army in the search of a good place where he would deal with his troumer. At his training it worked but as he was living the army after having fought at Irack, he come back home much worse than he left, as a way of finding his way back to the community which he felt disconected from he was employed in a bookshop where a man who claimed to have been his fathers friend took him in a life transforming process

Through the book Brake shares with us how the man, Tommy Flinn, who was a new emploee of the same bookshop transformed his life by introdusing him to four teachers who teaches him and us what life is like when you lead without a title.
